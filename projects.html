Technical Experience

Experience 1: Lumina Instruments / Onto Innovation

At Lumina/Onto, I work remotely to enhance product's software by implementing improvements and adding new features using C#. My role focuses on refining and optimizing the software to meet evolving needs. During the summer, I collaborated with engineers to test electronic circuit boards and power supply modules. After the testing phase, I contributed to updating and revising test procedures to improve accuracy and efficiency. Additionally, I configured computer systems by installing hardware components and software applications to support testing operations.

Experience 2: Cepheid

At Cepheid I was tasked with two projects during my internship. The first project involved collaborating with the firmware verification team to perform several tests on a company product. These tests consisted of functional tests, with optical and thermal calibration. The second project involved validating the manufacturing software, ensuring alignment between the test protocol and the software to prevent any gaps in functionality.
